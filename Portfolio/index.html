Speech Emotion Recognition

Speech emotion recognition (SER) analyzes speech patterns to identify emotions. It considers factors like pitch, volume, and rhythm to understand the emotional state behind someone's words. SER applications are growing, from improving human-computer interaction to analyzing customer sentiment. Imagine a virtual assistant that adjusts its tone based on your emotions, or a system that detects frustration in a call center conversation.
